About Us

who we are ?

dmt.gov.lk is a solution provider for srilankan citizens who are looking forward to obtain their driving license or to fix their driving license related issues

our objectives

  • Facilitate citizens with downloadable forms in relation to driving license provided by government organizations
  • provide safe and user friendly mechanisms for uploading information to the website
  • provide all types of application forms and submissions that are required in the process of obtaining the license
  • display exam schedules and latest news
  • provide all kind of information including necessary registration road safety policies and tips
